  Description:
    Abstract: 2012 305(b) assessed and 303(d) impaired hydrology features
    Purpose: Show all waters identified in the 2012 Virginia 305(b)/303(d) Water Quality Assessment Integrated Report.
    Supplemental_Information: Assessment determinations noted in this dataset are based on guidance from Virginia's 2012 Assessment Guidance Memo 11-2007 available at http://www.deq.virginia.gov/Portals/0/DEQ/Water/Guidance/112007.pdf.  This dataset has two supplemental GIS data files: the regionally significant VPDES facilities shapefile (va_12ir_vpdes_facilities.shp) and the monitoring stations shapefile (va_12ir_wqm_stations.shp).
